My passion for technology began with a simple idea: that great IT isn't just about fixing things, it's about empowering people.

As an IT professional, I thrive on solving complex problems and building robust environments that ensure business continuity for my clients. Whether it's through escalated break/fix support, system administration, or implementing cloud security solutions, my goal is always to deliver operational excellence. I believe in working smarter, not harder. That's why I've spent my career streamlining processes with intelligent automation and scripting, all to solve the high-priority issues that keep businesses running smoothly. At Systems Engineering, I've had the chance to lead critical projects, like the Cloud Security Task Force, where I took the reins on implementing security measures and auditing vulnerabilities. I'm also passionate about developing talent and have led training workshops to help my team members grow their skills. My commitment to excellence has been recognized with multiple awards, but the real reward is the satisfaction of a job well done. I'm always expanding my technical toolkit from Azure and AWS to virtualization and security tools. I'm currently pursuing my Bachelor's in Computer Science to continue exploring the exciting world of emergent AI and cutting-edge technology.
